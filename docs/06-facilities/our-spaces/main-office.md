# GSS Main Office (Thea Koerner House, Rm 452)

The GSS Main Office is the core workspace for executives and staff.

| Topic | Current guide status |

## Notes to Add

- Confirm who owns day-to-day office access administration.
- Document the current process for reporting repairs or safety concerns.
- Add only role-based contacts, not personal phone numbers or private access
  instructions.

{: .warning }
Do not publish door codes, alarm details, keys, lockbox locations, or other
access-sensitive information.

# FollowUpThen

FollowUpThen is a free email reminder service that requires no account or app. You forward or BCC any email to a time-encoded address and receive the message back as a reminder at that moment.

## How it works

Forward or BCC any email to `[time]@followupthen.com`. Examples:

| Address | When you'll be reminded |
|---------|------------------------|
| `friday@followupthen.com` | Next Friday morning |
| `2weeks@followupthen.com` | In two weeks |
| `sep1@followupthen.com` | September 1st |
| `jan2028@followupthen.com` | January 2028 |

The reminder arrives as an email with the original message attached.

## FO use case examples

- **Contract negotiations** — BCC a future date when a vendor contract is up for renegotiation or renewal.
- **Pending accounts** — Follow up on outstanding invoices, reimbursements, or cheque requisitions that haven't been confirmed.
- **Banking follow-ups** — Remind yourself to confirm a Plooto transfer cleared or a Vancity document was received.
